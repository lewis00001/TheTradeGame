The Trade Game

The game begins with the player having 20 of each of the 8 trade goods. The object of the game is to trade these goods to the trader who will pay the most for them. On some trades, the trader will pay the standard rate for each item traded, on other turns they will offer either a 4 or an 8 gold bonus for each item. They offer the same price for a particular good during the same turn number, so it is possible to memorize what they offer and complete the game with a perfect score in just 8 turns.
